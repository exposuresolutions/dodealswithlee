# GoHighLevel Outbound Voice AI Beta is HERE! (Full Setup + Compliance Walkthrough)

**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oIm1rx3RsXI
**Author:** Nuno Tavares | Automated Marketer

Here are the key features, setup steps, API/automation capabilities, and potential marketplace app ideas extracted from the GoHighLevel tutorial video:

**Key Features:**

* Outbound voice AI calling
* Compliance checks for outbound calling
* Know Your Customer (KYC) information required
* CNAM and Seek and Stir integration
* Valid captured legal opt-in required
* Respect for strict rate limits and DND (Do Not Disturb) hours
* Real-time compliance scanning
* Phone number specific calling limits (one call per day, no more than 4 calls in a rolling 14-day window)
* US numbers only
* Call rules (10am-6pm per context time zone)

**Setup Steps:**

* Complete compliance check on HighLevel support
* Register for an HP number
* Set up CNAM and Seek and Stir
* Create a valid captured legal opt-in
* Ensure proof of consent before making outbound calls
* Respect strict rate limits and DND hours
* Set up automation to ensure compliance
* Recreate bots to enable outbound calling

**API/Automation Capabilities:**

* Automation to ensure compliance with outbound calling rules
* Integration with CNAM and Seek and Stir
* Real-time compliance scanning
* API capabilities for custom integrations (not explicitly mentioned, but possible)

**Potential Marketplace App Ideas:**

* Compliance checker app to ensure users are meeting all requirements for outbound calling
* Automated opt-in generator to create valid captured legal opt-ins
* DND hour manager to ensure users are respecting strict rate limits and DND hours
* Call analytics app to track and analyze outbound call performance
* Integration with third-party services (e.g. phone number validation, address verification) to enhance compliance and accuracy
* Custom bot builder for outbound calling to simplify the process of creating compliant bots

Note: The video emphasizes the importance of compliance and caution when using outbound calling features, and encourages users to educate themselves on the laws and regulations surrounding outbound calling in their area.
