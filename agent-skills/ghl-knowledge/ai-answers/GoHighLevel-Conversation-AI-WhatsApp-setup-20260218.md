# GoHighLevel Conversation AI WhatsApp setup

*Researched: 2026-02-18 01:33*


## Groq (Llama 70B)

To set up GoHighLevel Conversation AI WhatsApp, follow these steps:

1. **Enable WhatsApp in Agency Settings**: Go to **Agency Settings** (gear icon in the top right corner) > **Channels** > **WhatsApp**. Toggle the switch to enable WhatsApp.
2. **Set up WhatsApp Business Account**: Go to **Agency Settings** > **Channels** > **WhatsApp** > **WhatsApp Business Account**. Follow the prompts to set up your WhatsApp Business Account.
3. **Configure WhatsApp Settings**: Go to **Agency Settings** > **Channels** > **WhatsApp** > **Settings**. Configure your WhatsApp settings, such as setting up a WhatsApp phone number and configuring message templates.
4. **Create a WhatsApp Conversation AI**: Go to **Conversations** > **AI** > **Create AI**. Select **WhatsApp** as the channel and choose a template or start from scratch.
5. **Configure AI Settings**: Configure your WhatsApp Conversation AI settings, such as setting up intents, entities, and responses.
6. **Test and Deploy**: Test your WhatsApp Conversation AI and deploy it to your WhatsApp channel.

**Menu Path:**

* Agency Settings: gear icon in the top right corner
* Channels: Agency Settings > Channels
* WhatsApp: Agency Settings > Channels > WhatsApp
* WhatsApp Business Account: Agency Settings > Channels > WhatsApp > WhatsApp Business Account
* Settings: Agency Settings > Channels > WhatsApp > Settings
* Conversations: left sidebar
* AI: Conversations > AI
* Create AI: Conversations > AI > Create AI

**Button Names:**

* Enable WhatsApp: toggle switch
* Set up WhatsApp Business Account: button
* Configure Settings: button
* Create AI: button
* Test and Deploy: buttons

Note: The setup process may vary depending on your GoHighLevel plan and configuration. If you're unsure about any step, refer to the GoHighLevel documentation or contact their support team.
