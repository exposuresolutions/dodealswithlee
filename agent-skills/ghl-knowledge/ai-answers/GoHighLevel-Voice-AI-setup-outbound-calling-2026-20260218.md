# GoHighLevel Voice AI setup outbound calling 2026

*Researched: 2026-02-18 01:32*


## Groq (Llama 70B)

To set up GoHighLevel Voice AI for outbound calling in 2026, follow these steps:

1. **Enable Voice AI features in Labs**:
	* Go to **Agency Settings** (gear icon in the top right corner) > **Labs**.
	* Scroll down to the **Voice AI** section and toggle the switch to **Enable**.
2. **Enable Voice AI token-based usage for sub-accounts**:
	* Go to the **Sub-account level** (select the sub-account you want to enable Voice AI for).
	* Click on **Settings** (gear icon) > **Voice AI**.
	* Toggle the switch to **Enable Voice AI token-based usage**.
3. **Activate Voice AI outbound calling**:
	* In the same **Voice AI** settings page, toggle the switch to **Enable Voice AI outbound calling**.
	* Note: This feature is marked as "coming soon" in the tutorial, so it might not be available yet. Check the GoHighLevel documentation for the latest updates.
4. **Set up Voice AI settings**:
	* Go to **Voice AI settings** (click on the **Voice AI** tab in the top navigation bar).
	* Explore the various settings and options available, such as **Voice AI token usage**, **Conversation support AI**, and **MCP support**.
5. **Configure Voice AI chat widget**:
	* Go to **Settings** (gear icon) > **Chat Widget**.
	* Activate the **Voice AI chat widget** for all channels.

Please note that the availability and functionality of these features might change over time. It's always a good idea to check the official GoHighLevel documentation and tutorials for the latest information and updates.
