# Logo Variations from Google (Gemini 2.5)

As a world-class brand designer specializing in premium real estate, I understand the critical balance between timeless elegance and cutting-edge appeal. Your brand, Do Deals With Lee™, represents significant achievement and trust, and the logo must reflect that gravitas while embracing a modern, dynamic aesthetic.

The client feedback is clear: Lee loves the neon glow, and the house icon *must* be distinct. My approach will integrate these preferences with sophisticated execution, ensuring each variation is not only visually stunning but also highly functional across all desired applications—from a 30-foot billboard to an embroidered polo and a custom neon sign. We're aiming for logos that don't just exist but *flow* and embody the brand's premium stature.

Here are four distinct variations designed to elevate Do Deals With Lee™ to a $10K+ showstopper status:

### 1. Urban Horizon Neon
**Image Prompt:** A premium real estate investment brand logo for "Do Deals With Lee™" set against a deep, luxurious black (#0A0A0A) background. The logo is rendered in a classic, true neon tube style, appearing as if it's a fabricated neon sign. The layout is stacked, reading top-to-bottom. "DO" is on the top left, glowing in vibrant orange neon (#FF6B00). Next to "DO", the distinct house icon (roofline with a window) glows brightly in modern cyan neon (#00E5FF). Below, "DEALS" is large and prominent, glowing in crisp cyan neon (#00E5FF). At the bottom, "WITH LEE" is contained within a sleek tag/badge shape, glowing in energetic orange neon (#FF6B00). The neon tubes have visible outlines, emitting a soft, realistic glow onto the surrounding black surface, with subtle reflections. The entire design is sharp, high-contrast, and easily readable from a distance, suitable for both large-scale signage and intricate embroidery, embodying modern luxury and trust.
**Color Breakdown:** DO=Orange (#FF6B00), House=Cyan (#00E5FF), DEALS=Cyan (#00E5FF), WITH LEE=Orange (#FF6B00), Background=Black (#0A0A0A)
**Why It Works:** This variation provides excellent contrast between the warm orange and cool cyan, fulfilling the critical requirement for the house icon to be a different color. The classic neon style is directly what Lee loves, while the color split maintains dynamism and readability.
**Best Application:** Ideal for an actual fabrication-ready neon sign, eye-catching digital banners, and bold "FOR SALE" signage.
