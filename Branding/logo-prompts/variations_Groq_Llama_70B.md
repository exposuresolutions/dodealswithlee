The neon signs should be set against a dark, gradient background that resembles a night sky, with the cityscape of Tampa, FL, subtly visible in the distance. The logo should be readable from 30 feet away and scalable for various merchandise.
**Color Breakdown:** DO=orange (#FF6B00), House=cyan (#00E5FF), DEALS=cyan (#00E5FF), WITH LEE=orange (#FF6B00), Background=black (#0A0A0A)
**Why It Works:** This color combination works because the contrast between cyan and orange creates a visually appealing and energetic vibe, perfect for a real estate investment brand. The classic neon style adds a touch of nostalgia and sophistication.
**Best Application:** This version shines most on signage, especially on a large FOR SALE sign or a billboard, where the neon effect can be fully appreciated.
