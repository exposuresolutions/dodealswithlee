Five Treatments, One Logo

Each variation uses the same logo shape. The difference is the color treatment — designed for specific use cases but all interchangeable.

Original White on Black

The foundation. Clean white logo on the signature black. This is the default for website headers, email signatures, and dark backgrounds.

Variation 3 — Gold Premium

Variation 4 — Orange Fire

Orange #FF6B00

Cyan-to-Gold Gradient

The showstopper. A gradient from cyan through to gold across the logo. This is the hero version — for billboards, large format prints, website hero sections, and statement merch pieces. Visible from a distance, stunning up close.

Merch Vision

Not Just a Logo on a Shirt — A Design Through It

The logo stays the same. But the brand colors flow through the garment as design elements — waves, gradients, accent lines. The logo sits within the design, not on top of it.

T-Shirt

Cyan wave flows across the torso. Logo centered on chest. Hem accent line.

Snapback

Logo front and center. Cyan-to-gold accent on brim edge.

Polo (Navy)

Gold embroidered logo left chest. Gold collar accent. Sleeve piping.

Dress Shirt

Dark cyan embroidered on pocket. Subtle cyan accent on cuffs.

Scarf

Black with gold + cyan woven stripes. Logo subtle in center.

Balaclava

Cyan logo on forehead. Subtle accent line. Cleveland winter essential.

The Philosophy

The logo is the anchor. The brand colors flow through the garment as design elements — accent lines on collars, cuffs, brims, and hems. Gradient waves across torsos. Gold piping on sleeves. The clothing IS the brand, not just a canvas for a logo. People see the colors and the style before they even read "Do Deals With Lee" — and that's the point.
