📋 What to Tell Your Sign Maker

Color: Ice Blue / Cyan LED Neon — HEX #00E5FF
Style: LED Flex Neon (not traditional glass)
Backing: Black acrylic or clear (for dark wall mount)
Font: Playfair Display Bold/ExtraBold (provide TTF file)
Size: 24-36 inches wide (typical podcast backdrop)
Dimmer: Yes — adjustable for camera lighting
Power: Standard wall plug with inline dimmer
Provide: SVG logo file for exact letter shapes
