The Merch Philosophy

The logo is NOT just printed on top of clothing. The brand colors and design elements flow through the product — like a wave, a flare, a streak of energy. When someone wears DDWL merch, the entire garment IS the brand.

The Neon Test

Every logo must pass the neon sign test: Can a sign maker trace these lines in LED neon tubing? Does it glow beautifully in #00E5FF cyan? Will it look incredible behind Lee on his podcast?

📋 Quick Decision Guide

Pick A (The Wave) if you want a modern, clean brand that feels like Nike or Under Armour meets real estate. Best for: younger audience, tech-forward image.

Pick B (The Flame) if you want aggressive energy that screams hustle and action. Best for: high-energy events, motivational brand, streetwear appeal.

Pick C (The Crown) if you want premium authority that says "I'm the king of this game." Best for: high-net-worth clients, luxury positioning, legacy brand.

💡 Neon Sign Compatibility

A: Simple clean lines = easiest to produce in neon. ✓ Best for neon
B: Angular lines work well. Flame detail may need simplification. ⚠ Good
C: Crown detail is intricate. May need simplified crown for neon. ⚠ Good with simplification
