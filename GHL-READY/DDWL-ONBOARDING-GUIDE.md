## PHASE 1: Day 1 — System Access & Setup

### 1.1 Google Workspace (G-Suite)

Your primary work platform. Replaces Office 365.

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Email** | your.name@dodealswithlee.com |
| **Login** | https://workspace.google.com |
| **Includes** | Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Docs, Google Sheets, Google Slides, Google Drive, Google Meet |
| **AI** | Gemini AI built-in for drafting, research, analysis |

**First Login Steps:**
1. Go to https://workspace.google.com
2. Sign in with your @dodealswithlee.com email
3. Use the temporary password provided by admin
4. **Change your password immediately**
5. **Enable 2-Factor Authentication** (Settings > Security > 2-Step Verification)
6. Set up your email signature (template will be in your Drive)
7. Download Gmail and Google Calendar apps on your phone

**Company Standard:** All documents are Google Docs/Sheets/Slides by default. If you have Microsoft Office, you can still open Google files, but create new documents in Google format.

**Why G-Suite over Office 365?**
- Saves license costs
- Better real-time collaboration
- Gemini AI integration
- Auto-backup to Google Drive
- Works on any device, any browser

5. **Recording** — Calls may be recorded for training. Inform callers per state law
6. **Documentation** — Log every interaction in GHL. If it's not in GHL, it didn't happen

## PHASE 3: 30/60/90 Day Development Plan

### 30-Day Checkpoint (Month 1)

**Goal:** Learn the systems, understand the business, start contributing.

| Area | Target |
|------|--------|
| **Systems** | All logins working, comfortable navigating GHL |
| **Knowledge** | Understand DDWL's business model and offerings |
| **Skills** | Can handle basic tasks independently |
| **Communication** | Know the phone scripts, comfortable on calls |
| **Integration** | Building relationships with team members |

**Review:** Self-assessment + manager review meeting.

### 60-Day Checkpoint (Month 2)

**Goal:** Working independently, hitting initial targets.

| Area | Target |
|------|--------|
| **Independence** | Handling core responsibilities without supervision |
| **Performance** | Meeting initial KPI targets |
| **Advanced Skills** | Started advanced training modules |
| **Feedback** | Positive 360 feedback from team |
| **Initiative** | Identifying improvements and suggesting solutions |

**Review:** KPI review + 360 feedback + advanced training assessment.

### 90-Day Graduation (Month 3)

**Goal:** Full performance. Probation complete.

| Area | Target |
|------|--------|
| **Performance** | All KPI targets met or exceeded |
| **Autonomy** | Fully independent in role |
| **Culture** | Integrated into team, living DDWL values |
| **Growth** | Long-term development plan created |
| **Decision** | Probation passed → Full team member |

**Review:** Final review with Lee and/or Krystle. Outcomes:
- ✅ **Passed** — Welcome to the team permanently
- ⏳ **Extended** — More time needed, specific goals set
- ❌ **Exit** — Not the right fit
