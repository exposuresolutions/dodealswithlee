Your 30/60/90 Day Journey

What success looks like at each milestone

Days 1–7: Foundation

Week 1

Get all systems set up. Meet the team. Understand the business model. Start training.

  • All logins working
  • Completed team introductions
  • Reviewed company overview materials
  • Started GHL training

Days 8–30: Learning

Month 1

Deep training on your role. Shadow experienced team members. Start handling tasks with supervision.

  • Completed all training modules
  • Handling basic tasks independently
  • Know the phone scripts
  • First 30-day review passed

Days 31–60: Building

Month 2

Working independently. Hitting initial targets. Contributing to team goals.

  • Independent task completion
  • Meeting KPI targets
  • Positive team feedback
  • Advanced training started

Days 61–90: Performing

Month 3

Full performance. Probation review. Transition to permanent team member.

  • All targets met or exceeded
  • Probation review passed
  • Long-term development plan set
  • Full team member status
