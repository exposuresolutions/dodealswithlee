30-Day Checkpoint

First review — are you on track?

Day 30

Self-Assessment

Complete your 30-day self-assessment. Rate your confidence in each system and skill area. Identify what you need more training on.

Manager Review Meeting

Sit down with your manager to review your first 30 days. Topics:

  • What’s going well?
  • Where do you need support?
  • Are you hitting your initial targets?
  • Feedback on team integration

30-Day Targets Review

Review against your 30-day targets (set during Week 1). Adjust 60-day targets based on progress.

Targets will be defined by your manager during orientation.

Personal Development Plan Update

Update your PDP with new goals, skills to develop, and resources needed for the next 30 days.

Your 30/60/90 Day Journey

What success looks like at each milestone

Days 1–7: Foundation

Week 1

Get all systems set up. Meet the team. Understand the business model. Start training.

  • All logins working
  • Completed team introductions
  • Reviewed company overview materials
  • Started GHL training

Days 8–30: Learning

Month 1

Deep training on your role. Shadow experienced team members. Start handling tasks with supervision.

  • Completed all training modules
  • Handling basic tasks independently
  • Know the phone scripts
  • First 30-day review passed

Days 31–60: Building

Month 2

Working independently. Hitting initial targets. Contributing to team goals.

  • Independent task completion
  • Meeting KPI targets
  • Positive team feedback
  • Advanced training started

Days 61–90: Performing

Month 3

Full performance. Probation review. Transition to permanent team member.

  • All targets met or exceeded
  • Probation review passed
  • Long-term development plan set
  • Full team member status
